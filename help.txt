=== Keyboard Shortcuts ===

Enter:          Play song from current pattern
Ctrl + Enter:   Play pattern & loop 
Shift + Enter:  Play pattern from current step
Escape:         Stop playback
Space:          Enter record / edit mode

Edit mode keys:
Delete:         Clear note on current step
Z-M:            Enter notes in lower octave
Q-P:            Enter notes in upper octaves
