=== Keyboard Shortcuts ===

Space:          Play / stop
Ctrl + Space:   Enable pattern loop and play
Shift + Space:  Play from current position
Enter:          Play line by line
Escape:         Enter record and edit mode


Edit mode keys:
Delete:         Clear note on current step
Z-M:            Enter notes in lower octave
Q-P:            Enter notes in upper octaves
